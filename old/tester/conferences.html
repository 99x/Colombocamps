Conferences

The community has been constantly arranging many camps and have invited many international and local speakers to share experiences and knowledge for the betterment of the Sri Lankan IT Industry. Following the success of these events, it was decided to take a step ahead by providing the IT enthusiasts and professionals with an opportunity to take part in a large scale open event with the participation of world renowned professionals and the local masterminds.




Software Product Engineering Conference

The "Software Product Engineering Conference - Colombo" organized by the Colombo Camps Community will feature a panel of international speakers along with a Microsoft Evangelist sharing their knowledge on Software Engineering practices.

Official Website : www.spec.lk |


Colombo Agile Conference

Following the Colombo Agile Camp, the Colombo Agile Conference was held on the 23rd January 2013 with an objective of spreading knowledge on agile practices in the Sri Lankan ICT landscape. This was an initiative taken by the "Colombo Agile Community" (part of the Colombo Camps Community), an independent association with a common aim of enhancing and improving skills of local professionals towards Agile Methodologies. Internationally recognized Scrum and Agile Professionals presented a range of related topics right from basics to advanced areas at this conference. Featuring the expertise of world class Agile Practitioners and industry professionals alike, this gave a comprehensive hands-on experience to the world of Agile.

Official Website : www.colomboagileconference.com |
